Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Rep Gate (“Application”) is provided by Rep Gate Team (“Service Provider”) as an ad-supported and fitness-focused experience. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the options you have.

Information We Collect

Rep Gate stores most data directly on your device so the app can calculate credits, track exercise progress, and determine whether blocked apps can launch. The Application and its integrated services may collect:

  • Screen-Time & Rep Data: Remaining credits, rep counts, difficulty settings, debt generated by ad skips, and daily ad usage (stored locally via SharedPreferences).
  • Blocked-App & Usage Stats: Package names you mark as blocked plus foreground-app information supplied by Android’s Usage Access API so we know when to lock an app.
  • Sensor & Camera Inputs: Camera frames and motion data processed on-device to count reps. We do not transmit or store raw images on our servers.
  • Notification & Overlay Preferences: Permission states and notification scheduling for reminders and overlays.
  • App Diagnostics: Crash logs, analytics, and device metadata collected by Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics to improve reliability.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Mobile Ads SDK data (ad unit interactions, device/identifier info) required to deliver rewarded ads.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Determine whether a blocked app should remain locked or can relaunch.
  • Calculate exercise credits, adjust difficulty, and notify you when time is low.
  • Provide camera-based exercise feedback and rep detection.
  • Serve rewarded ads that let you skip a workout in exchange for rep debt.
  • Send reminders/alerts via local notifications.
  • Monitor performance and stability using Firebase analytics/crash reports.

Permissions We Request

  • Camera & Motion Sensors: Required for pose detection and rep counting.
  • Usage Access & Accessibility: Needed to monitor which apps are in the foreground and enforce locks.
  • Overlay / Draw Over Apps: Enables the App Blocked screen on top of other applications.
  • Notifications & Background Service: Lets Rep Gate send reminders and run the monitoring service.

Third-Party Services

The Application integrates the following providers, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google LLC): Usage metrics and crash diagnostics.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Rewarded interstitial ads used to grant temporary screen-time credits.
  • Google ML Kit Pose Detection: On-device machine-learning models for exercise tracking.

Data Retention & Deletion

Screen-time credits, blocked app lists, and exercise stats remain on your device until you remove the app or reset data via the in-app settings. Firebase and AdMob data are retained per Google’s standard policies. You may request deletion of analytics data tied to your account by emailing us with the details needed to identify your records.

Advertising

Rep Gate uses rewarded ads only. When you choose to skip a workout by watching an ad, Google Mobile Ads collects device identifiers, engagement data, and other signals necessary to deliver the advertisement and prevent fraudulent activity. Rep Gate never shares your exercises, blocked apps, or credits with advertisers.

Children’s Privacy

Rep Gate is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us so we can delete it.

Security

We apply encryption for network calls (HTTPS), rely on Google’s vetted SDKs, and minimize external transmission of personal data. Still, no method is 100% secure, so we encourage you to keep your device updated and protected.

Your Choices

  • Disable or revoke permissions (camera, usage stats, notifications) at any time via system settings.
  • Reset all stored data from Settings → Reset inside the app.
  • Request deletion of analytics/diagnostic data by emailing us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Rep Gate evolves. The “Last Updated” date reflects the latest changes; material updates will be highlighted in-app or on our website.
